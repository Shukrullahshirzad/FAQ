What keeps you motivated during challenging projects?

  1. Clear goals and a sense of purpose.
  2. Celebrating small wins along the way.
  3. Support from a strong team.
  4. Reminding myself of the positive impact the project will have.

How do you stay focused and productive?

  • Breaking tasks into smaller, manageable steps.
  • Setting daily priorities and sticking to them.
  • Taking short breaks to recharge.
  • Staying organized and tracking progress.
